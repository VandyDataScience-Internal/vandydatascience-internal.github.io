This year, we are considering applications for EMs, Developers, and Workshop Creators. EMs and Developers are project-facing positions, while Workshop Creators are not.

Our project-facing positions (EMs, Developers) will run throughout the duration of our project cycle (November through April). Projects are around a 6-8 hour/week time commitment for EMs and a 4-6 hour/week time commitment for Developers.

As an EM, you will be responsible for:

  • Designing the tech stack for your team, including choosing a framework and/or library as well as the deliverable format
  • Leading product discovery meetings with stakeholders
  • Facilitating weekly meetings with your team
  • Designing and presenting your team's final poster presentation
  • Preparing a technical hand-over guide for future teams

As a Developer, you will be responsible for:

  • Creating the project!
  • Data exploration, cleaning, and analysis as well as model building and evaluation
  • Coding the deliverable
  • Attending weekly meetings with your team
  • Presenting your team's final poster presentation
  • Preparing a technical hand-over guide for future teams

Workshop Creators will design and lead an hour-long workshop on a topic related to data science and AI/ML to the broader Vanderbilt community.

As a Workshop Creator, you will be responsible for:

  • Choosing a workshop topic
  • Creating the workshop
  • Advertising the workshop
  • Leading the workshop
