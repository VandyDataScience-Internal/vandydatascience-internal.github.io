We're accepting VDS executive board applications for the 2024-25 school year! Descriptions for our open positions can be found below. All applications are due Tuesday, March 19, 2024 at 11:59pm.

Co-Presidents (2)

  • Oversees all club operations, leads executive board meetings, spearheads member recruitment, and ensures the club's mission is fulfilled through projects and workshops

Director of Finance

  • For reporting purposes only, is listed as Treasurer in AnchorLink
  • Manages the club's budget, oversees funding for events and supplies, and ensures that financial resources support all club activities
  • Submits annual application for Student Services Funding in March/April 2025

Director of Communications

  • For reporting purposes only, is listed as Secretary in AnchorLink
  • Creates advertisement materials for workshops as well as the final showcase, in addition to managing internal and external communication such as meeting minutes and announcements

Director of Workshops

  • Coordinates monthly educational workshops, working closely with the workshop team to plan and execute sessions that enhance members' data science skills

Director of Projects

  • Identifies and partners with non-profits for data science projects, designs data science projects over the summer, and maintains regular progress check-ins with project team leads

Please submit your application using the Google Form below. Here, you'll have the opportunity to indicate which position(s) you are applying for.
