Welcome

We're so glad you're interested in applying for one of our 3 member positions this year! On this site, you can find all the information you need to complete this year's coding challenge.

We will be accepting applications for two project-facing positions:

  • Engineering Managers (1-2 people per project)
  • Developers (~5 people per project)

Projects are valuable if you are interested in:

  • Making a difference to the world with innovative nonprofits
  • Gaining hands-on experience with data science and machine learninng
  • Being a part of a close-knit team of passionate students

Projects will span from November through April of the 2023-24 school year. We are excited to partner with the following organizations and nonprofits this year:

  • HomeMore
  • ThriftSmart
  • Minnie's Food Pantry
  • Nashville Zoo
  • Land Trust for Tennessee
  • Act To Change

Additionally, we will be accepting applications for Workshop Creators:

  • As a Workshop Creator, you will have the unique opportunity to create a workshop on an topic that excites you and share it with the Vanderbilt community

The same assessment will be used for all 3 positions. In your final submission, you will have the opportunity to indicate which of the 3 positions you are interested in applying for. You are free to apply to as many positions as you'd like!
