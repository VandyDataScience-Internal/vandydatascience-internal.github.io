Welcome

We're so glad you're interested in applying for one of our project roles this year! On this site, you can find all the information you need to complete your EM/Developer assessment.

Projects are very valuable if you are interested in:

  • Applying for future internships
  • Improving your resume
  • Gaining real, relevant experience for a data-related career

Projects will span from November through April of the 2023-24 school year. We are excited to partner with a variety of organizations and nonprofits this year. All the available projects can be found on our main website.

We will be accepting applications for two positions:

  • Engineering Managers (1-2 people per project)
  • Developers (~5 people per project)

The same assessment will be used for both positions. In your final submission, you will be able to indicate whether you are also interested in applying for an EM role. All EM applications will also be automatically considered for Developer roles.

Please don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions during the application process. We're looking forward to a great year!
